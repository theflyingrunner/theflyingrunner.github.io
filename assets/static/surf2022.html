Summer Undergraduate Research Fellowship

Saron Bhoopathy

Abstract

During summer 2022, I was accepted into the SURF program at Purdue and did research with the Digital Agriculture Discovery (DAD) Lab. The skills I gained from working with Prof. James Goppert as part of Vertically Integrated Projects (VIP) helped me contribute to the needs of the DAD Lab. My project for the summer was to develop a software-in-the-loop simulation for crop-scouting drones and rovers. I primarily used Robot Operating System 2 (ROS2) and Gazebo for simulation. I also dabbled in object-detection algorithms like YOLO, which introduced me to Deep Learning neural networks. At the end of the summer, I presented my research at the SURF Symposium.
